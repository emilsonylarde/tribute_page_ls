La Signora

A tribute to the fallen Fatui Harbinger

An old member of the Fatui that rules the land of Snezhnaya and serves the Tsaritsa of Snezhnaya with the best of her abilities

Trivias:

  • She is the Crimson Witch of Flames
  • Also known as Rosalyne-Kruzchka Lohefalter and by her codename "The Fair Lady".
  • Was the Eighth of the Eleven Fatui Harbingers
  • She is the first Harbinger to appear in the game when she ambushes and overpowers Venti to steal his Gnosis
  • Was an ordinary young woman from Mondstadt who studied at Sumeru Akademiya 500 years ago.
  • When her lover Rostam died during the cataclysm, Rosalyne was consumed by
    anguish and hatred and used the art of liquid fire to transform her body into that of living flame,
    thereafter becoming known as the Crimson Witch of Flame.
  • Wields a Cryo Delusion and can also use fire-based powers.
  • Described as someone who considers the consequences of her actions yet also mentions that she uses
    lethal force against new Fatui.
  • Describes those harmed by her actions in the story as "necessary sacrifices" and "nobodies."

    • "But that light is not the dawn, dear Rosalyne. That is a sea of flame that will consume everything."
